# Specification: AI Accuracy and Robustness Overhaul

## Goal
The AI, particularly the `MastermindAI`, should be capable of hitting any stationary target within 4 shots on any terrain generated by the `terrain.js` module.

## Requirements
1.  **Test-Driven Development:** A comprehensive test suite (`js/aiAccuracy.test.js`) must be the source of truth for AI performance.
2.  **Physics Parity:** The simulation used by the AI (`findBestShot`) must perfectly match the game's actual physics logic in `tank.js`.
3.  **Terrain Awareness:** The AI must intelligently handle blocked lines of sight, opting for high lobs when necessary and avoiding terrain collisions.
4.  **Robust Learning:** The history-based interpolation must converge on the correct power even with non-linear environmental factors (like wind varying with height, if implemented later).
5.  **Self-Preservation:** AI should avoid firing shots that result in immediate self-harm (e.g., shooting into a nearby wall).

## Success Criteria
- `MastermindAI` hit rate >= 90% within 4 shots in the automated benchmark.
- No "infinite loops" of AI firing the same bad shot over and over.
- AI correctly lobs over high terrain obstacles.
